📄 Universal Core

These fields exist on every profile, regardless of profession. Think of this as the "base layer" that everyone fills out.

🆔 Identity
ID
Unique identifier for each person
Name
Display name shown on their profile
Status
Whether the profile is active or inactive
Created at
When the profile was first created
Updated at
When the profile was last edited
Onboarding source
How they joined — founder invite, referral, or self-signup
Profile completeness
A score from 0 to 1 showing how filled-out the profile is
Verification
Trust level — how many credits and peer endorsements they have
👤 Personal
Gender
Male, Female, Non-binary, or Prefer not to say
Age
Used for casting filters and experience-level calibration
Languages spoken
All languages the person works in
Regional background
Cultural root — Telangana, Coastal Andhra, Rayalaseema, etc.
Bio
Free-text self-description — fully searchable by the engine
📍 Location
Current city
Where they currently live
State
State within the country
Country
Country of residence
Willing to travel
Other cities they are open to working in
💼 Professional
Primary craft
Their main profession with a specific subcraft
Secondary crafts
Other crafts they also practice
Experience (years)
Total years working in their primary craft
Experience level
Newcomer → Emerging → Mid-level → Senior → Veteran → Legend
Availability
Type of work, whether currently available, and next free date
Looking for
The types of opportunities they want
🔍 Discovery & History
Tags (self)
Free-text identity tags they assign themselves
Projects
Formal credits with role, year, banner, and proof links
Work samples
Portfolio links, reels, and showcase pieces
Education & training
Film schools, workshops, apprenticeships they've completed
Endorsements
Peer endorsements from collaborators (coming in v2)
Media
Profile photo, cover image, and portfolio images
